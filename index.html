Welcome to The Spa

The Spa on Port Royal Sound is a hidden treasure, located in a secluded area of Hilton Head Island, SC, nestled between two historic parks — Fish Haul Park and Mitchellville Park.

The community consists of 268 condos, 42 one-bedroom/one-bath villas, and 226 two-bedroom/two-bath villas. Amenities include an indoor heated pool, hot tub, fitness room, two outdoor pools, tennis courts, a basketball court, picnic and grilling areas, a children’s playground, and a natural beach on the Port Royal Sound within a bird sanctuary.

Key Rules at a Glance

The essentials every resident, guest, and renter should know on arrival. For the full rules, see the complete Rules page.

Pet Policy

The Spa, HPR does not allow pets on the property. Service / Emotional Support Animals (ESAs) are allowed, but must be pre-registered with the HOA Office at least 10 days prior to arrival. Required documents include the submittal form, shot records, and a physician’s letter for ESAs.

Parking

Only two vehicles are allowed per unit. Every vehicle must display a valid parking pass or registered barcode sticker. Short-term guests: $25 per vehicle (1–89 days). Long-term guests: $175 for up to two vehicles (90+ days). Park straight against the blocks; respect Fire Lanes and Trash Pickup Zones.

Quiet Hours

10:00 PM – 8:00 AM. Avoid noise that can be heard beyond the perimeter of your unit. No loud music, furniture moving, or excessive noise during these hours.

Pool Hours

Indoor Pool / Fitness Center: 8:00 AM – 10:00 PM (requires blue-handled key). Outdoor Pools: 8:00 AM – Sunset (regulated by the state of South Carolina). Children under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian.

Prohibited Vehicles

No RVs, motorhomes, travel trailers, pop-up campers, boats, jet skis, kayaks, canoes, trailers, commercial vans/box trucks, or e-bikes. Violations result in towing or HOA fines.

Trash & Walkways

Bag all household trash and place it only in designated dumpsters — never on walkways. No bulk items, furniture, or construction debris near dumpsters. Do not hang towels or clothing over railings.

Violations of community rules may result in fines; see the Fine Schedule (PDF).
