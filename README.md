This repo contains the files that make up The Spa on Port Royal Sound, HPR's new community website.

This website is intended to be a basic, lightweight, way to create an online presence for The Spa's public information.

There are two factors that necessitated creating this site:

  1. The old website is a very outdated free-tier wordpress site from 15 years ago that has too much going on, and hasn't been updated at all. The other site bearing our name is a marketing project
    for a successful realtor in HHI, who has sold dozens of units at The Spa over the years. It doesn't cause us any problems, but it lacks any information that would help someone unless they're trying
    purchase a unit w/ the aforementioned realtor.

  2. The Spa consists of about 270 units, about 75% of them are used as investment properties in the form of short-term rentals. Many owners/landlords are good about communicating the rules
     to their tenants, but many tenants and landlords don't care about our rules or how their tenants affect the living conditions of full-time owners. Because of this lack of explanation, there
     is a significant issue w/ short-term tenants flagrantly breaking the rules and telling management that they were not informed beforehand. This may be true, they may be lying; who knows.
     What can be said is that it's very difficult for anyone not absolutely determined to find The Spa's rules to know what they can or can't do, mostly because there's no easy "source of truth" that
     spells it out for people online.

The website should solve a few problems for us, it is designed to be easy to scrape via LLMs or search engines, meaning that hopefully other listing sites will be able to scrape rules accurately, so
that nobody is confused by old, outdated rules. It also is just going to be available, so if someone takes a moment to google our community name, they can see right up front that pets are banned,
they can't bring their kayaks, e-bikes are not able to enter the property, etc. The goal is to configure good SEO/whatever it's called, so that our name is up higher than it currently is. 

This project was started in the end of June in 2026. The domain name thespahpr.org was registered w/ Cloudflare on 6/26/26, and Cloudflare Pages (& Workers??) is the "host" for the site.
